# Privacy Policy for Ingredients Scanner

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Ingredients Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when you use the mobile application.

## 1. Data We Process

The app processes product and ingredient information you scan, such as:
- Barcode values
- Ingredient text recognized from photos
- Product details returned by OpenFoodFacts
- Local app settings (language, dietary preferences)
- Local scan history

## 2. Camera Permission

Ingredients Scanner requests `android.permission.CAMERA` to:
- Scan product barcodes
- Capture ingredient label images for OCR analysis

The camera is used only when you initiate scanning features.

## 3. Photo/Media Access

If you choose images from your gallery, the app accesses selected photos only for ingredient/barcode analysis.

## 4. Internet Use

The app uses the internet to fetch public product information from OpenFoodFacts when scanning barcodes.

## 5. Data Storage

Scan history and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not require account creation.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Product lookup requests may be sent to third-party services required for app functionality (for example, OpenFoodFacts) when you perform scans that need online lookup.

## 7. Data Deletion

You can delete your local scan history and settings from inside the app. Uninstalling the app also removes local app data from your device.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
